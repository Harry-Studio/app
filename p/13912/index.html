Esay Period - Elegant Period Tracking App

Created2026-01-01|Updated2025-12-25|iPhone|Health

Esay Period is a period tracking and management app designed for women, built with native SwiftUI technology to provide a simple, private, and reliable cycle tracking experience.

Highlights

  • Cycle calendar with smart prediction
  • Quick log for start/end and notes
  • Local and email reminders, custom time
  • iCloud private sync plus JSON import/export
  • Chinese/English switch, ad-free and tracking-free

Why choose it

  • Native SwiftUI experience, smooth and fast
  • Data stays in your private iCloud, encrypted end-to-end
  • Free to use, no ads or subscriptions
  • Clear stats: average cycle, shortest/longest, countdown to next period
